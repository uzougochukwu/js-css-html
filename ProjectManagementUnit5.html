Figure 1 should be adapted to include the 'signal to noise ratio' of the intended outcome. For example if you are using an email application, you will eventually ignore warnings of 'external email address'.

Usability is not just about the interface, but about the underlying work process that you are following. In 2020, Citibank accidentally paid back the entirety of a $900 million loan, rather than just an $8 million installment. One failing here, is that the the program even allowed the bank employees to pay the entire loan all at once. The user experience has to be matched to the workflow.
